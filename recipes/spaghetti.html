Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup shortening
  • 1 large onion, chopeed
  • lots of clove garlic, chopped
  • 1 tin tomato soup (10oz)
  • 1 tin tomates (19oz)
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp pepper
  • 1/8 tsp allspice
  • 1/8 tsp chili powder
  • 1/4 tsp thyme
  • 1/3 cup chopped parsley (optional)
  • 1 bay leaf

Steps

  1. Pan fry first 4 ingredients
  2. Add remaining ingredients
  3. Add browned meatballs
  4. Heat thoroghly
  5. During last 20 minutes prepare spaghetti noodles
  6. Serve sauce and meatballs over spaghetti noodles
